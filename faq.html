Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of cars do you offer for rent?

We offer a wide range of luxury vehicles, including sedans, SUVs, sports cars, and exotic vehicles. Whether you're looking for a sleek Mercedes-Benz or a powerful Ferrari, we have it all.

2. How can I purchase a car from Luxury Lane Motors?

You can browse our inventory online or visit our showroom. Once you find your dream car, our sales team will assist you with financing, paperwork, and delivery.

3. What are your rental terms and conditions?

Our rental terms include a minimum age requirement of 25, a valid driver's license, and a security deposit. For full details, please review our Rental Agreement.

4. Do you offer international delivery for purchased vehicles?

Yes, we offer international shipping services. Contact our sales team for a customized quote based on your location.

5. Can I extend my rental period?

Absolutely! Simply contact us at least 24 hours before your rental period ends, and we'll arrange an extension for you.
